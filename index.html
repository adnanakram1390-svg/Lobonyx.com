About Our Academy

Developing Future Football Stars

LOBONYX Sports Academy is a premier football training facility dedicated to developing young athletes between ages 8-16. Our mission is to provide professional football training that focuses on technical skills, tactical awareness, physical conditioning, and character development.

We believe that every young player has potential, and with the right guidance, training, and environment, that potential can be transformed into excellence both on and off the field.

Our specialized programs are designed to nurture talent, build confidence, and instill a lifelong love for football.
